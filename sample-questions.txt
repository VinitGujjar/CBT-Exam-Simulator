Section: Physics
What is the boiling point of water at standard atmospheric pressure?
A) 50 degree Celsius
B) 75 degree Celsius
C) 100 degree Celsius
D) 125 degree Celsius
Answer: C
Explanation: At standard atmospheric pressure, water boils at 100 degree Celsius.

Section: Electrical
Which material is the best conductor of electricity?
A) Silver
B) Copper
C) Aluminium
D) Iron
Answer: A
Explanation: Silver has the highest electrical conductivity among common metals.

Section: Units
What is the SI unit of power?
A) Newton
B) Joule
C) Watt
D) Pascal
Answer: C
Explanation: Power is the rate of doing work. Its SI unit is watt, where one watt equals one joule per second.
